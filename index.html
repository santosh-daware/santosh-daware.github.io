“Research is the distance between an idea and its realization”: David Sarnoff

Hello, I'm Santosh Vasant Daware


I am a Ph.D. scholar at IIT Bombay and Monash University, currently studying the behavior of 2D materials using colloidal analogues . I am currently pursuind PhD under the guidance of Prof. Guruswamy Kumaraswamy (IIT Bombay), Dr. Prabhakar Ranganthan (Monash University) and Dr. Amelia Liu (Monash University). Previously, I spent over 6 years as a Scientist and Team Lead at Tata Research Development and Design Centre. My research experience spans diverse fields, including formulation design, mineral processing, composite manufacturing, and e-waste recycling . I have both fundamental and applied research experience, having worked with prestigious clients in the automobile and biomedical industries.


I have successfully developed technologies, scaled up products and processes from TRL 1 to TRL 3. I am a motivated and enthusiastic team player, known for my ability to work collaboratively in international, cross-domain, and cross-academia-industry settings. My passion for research is matched by my commitment to fostering successful collaborations and achieving impactful results.


I completed my masters in Chemical Engineering by Research at IIT Madras under the guidance of Prof. Basavaraja Gurappa.


Beyond research, I enjoy playing sports like football, badminton, and running.
